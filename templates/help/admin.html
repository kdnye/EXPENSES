Administrator Guide

Manage access, role permissions, and policy alignment so expense reporting stays secure and consistent.

User approvals and permissions

Administrators control who can access the portal and what each user can do.

  • Employee: create, edit, and submit personal expense reports.
  • Supervisor: review assigned reports and approve or reject with comments.
  • Administrator: manage users, permissions, and platform settings.

During account approval, verify contact details, assign the correct role, and require strong passwords.

Policy controls and settings

Use the settings pages to keep category options, reimbursement rules, and review expectations current. When policy updates occur, notify supervisors so approvals remain consistent across teams.

  • Review expense categories and thresholds on a regular cadence.
  • Audit rejected-report comments for recurring issues that indicate policy confusion.
  • Deactivate or adjust accounts promptly when employees change teams or leave the company.

Audit readiness

Encourage teams to attach clear receipts and complete business-purpose notes. Consistent documentation shortens approvals and improves readiness for internal or external audits.
